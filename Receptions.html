Receptions

Welcome to the Receptions page! Here you can find detailed info about how Receptions work in Library of Ruina.

What Are Receptions?

Receptions are the core battle encounters in Library of Ruina, where invited Guests arrive at the Library to face off against the Librarians in combat. Each Reception is themed, story-driven, and escalates the narrative and gameplay complexity as you progress.

How Receptions Are Initiated

To initiate a Reception, the player must burn books obtained from previous battles. Burning a book reveals its contents and may yield other books or new Key Pages and Combat Pages. Certain combinations of burned books unlock new Guests who are then invited to a Reception.

  • Book Burning: Unlocks potential Receptions by revealing hidden Invitations.
  • Invitations: Each Reception requires a specific book (or set of books) as an offering to summon Guests.

Reception Structure

Once a Reception begins, the player chooses a team of Librarians to engage in battle against the invited Guests. Each team is equipped with Key Pages (which function like character loadouts) and Combat Pages (cards used during battle).

  • Pre-Battle Preparation: Choose your team, assign pages, and prepare for the specific Guests' mechanics.
  • Combat: The battle plays out using a dynamic speed dice and clash system where positioning, timing, and deck synergy are crucial.

Rewards and Progression

Winning a Reception rewards you with new books—sometimes rare or unique ones—and occasionally unlocks new story Receptions. Defeating certain key Guests advances the plot and opens access to new Floors or Abnormality Battles.

  • Books: Used to strengthen your team or unlock future Receptions.
  • Key Pages and Combat Pages: Customize Librarians with powerful new builds.

Story and Difficulty Scaling

Receptions increase in difficulty as the game progresses. Later Receptions may feature:

  • Multiple waves of enemies
  • Unique passive abilities
  • Stage gimmicks or battle conditions

Understanding enemy patterns and deck optimization becomes vital to victory. Every battle is both a test of strategy and a continuation of the Library’s dark tale.

Tips for Success

  • Read enemy passives carefully before battle.
  • Experiment with different Key Pages to counter specific strategies.
  • Use Floor Abilities and Librarian synergy to your advantage.

With every successful Reception, the Library grows stronger, and the secrets of the world outside are slowly revealed—one book at a time.
