Abnormalities

Abnormality Suppressions are unique, lore-rich combat encounters that take place in the Lower Floors of the Library. These battles revolve around suppressing Abnormalities — mysterious, often horrifying entities born from distorted human emotions and thoughts. Each Abnormality presents a different challenge, both narratively and mechanically.

How They Work

Unlike standard Receptions, Suppressions often involve special rules, phases, and gimmicks that test a player's adaptability and understanding of the game's deeper mechanics. They also serve as a reflection of the Librarians who face them, unlocking powerful E.G.O pages and progressing the story of the Library's internal departments.

Suppressions are unlocked progressively as the player advances through the main story and completes emotional thresholds in the Lower Floors (e.g., Yesod, Netzach, Hod, etc.). Each floor has its own set of Abnormalities that must be faced and suppressed in order to reach the floor's realization stage.

Key Features of Suppressions

  • Unique Mechanics: Each Abnormality has signature behaviors, resistances, and patterns that require tailored strategies to overcome.
  • Multiple Phases: Some Suppressions involve evolving phases, where the Abnormality changes form, abilities, or even battlefield rules.
  • Emotion Management: Managing your emotion levels is crucial, as both player and enemy powers escalate based on emotional energy.
  • E.G.O Gifts: Defeating an Abnormality may grant E.G.O gifts—permanent equipment that strengthens a Librarian with passive effects.

Floor Realizations

Successfully suppressing all Abnormalities on a floor unlocks its Realization battle — a climactic fight that symbolizes the emotional growth and resolution of that floor's Librarians. These fights are not only mechanically challenging but are heavily tied into the narrative development of the floor’s Sephirah.

Strategic Importance

Mastering Abnormality Suppressions is vital for preparing your team to face the Library’s most difficult Guests, especially during the final acts of the story. The E.G.O pages and gifts obtained can significantly improve a team's synergy and survivability.
