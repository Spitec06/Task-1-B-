Guests

Welcome to the Guests page! Here you can find detailed info about the Guests in Library of Ruina.

Rats

Canard

The Rats are low-level street thugs who inhabit the Backstreets. Consisting of people who have failed to achieve their dreams and can only focus on daily survival, groups of Rats are far too disorganized to be considered formal Syndicates, and are not affiliated with any other large factions.

Yun's Office

Canard

Yun’s Office is a Grade 7 Office comprised of low-ranking Fixers. Just one of the countless Fixer Offices trying to survive in the City, Yun’s Office is among the less fortunate. Struggling just to cover basic expenses, Yun's Fixers are forced to take on dull tasks like looking for lost cats, and this has strained relations between Yun and his Fixers.

Brotherhood Of Iron

Canard

The Brotherhood of Iron is a Syndicate composed of members using full prosthetic body replacements, wanting to focus on gaining money without the worry of biological conditions like hunger or disease. However, despite the combat advantage this gives them, their new bodies have only created a new set of problems for the Syndicate's members, from lack of bodily senses to mechanical maintenance issues, as they are quickly discovering.

Hook office

Canard

"The Hook Office is a murder-for-hire Office composed of former Syndicate members, known for its brutal conduct and capacity for messy jobs like contract killings and gruesome executions.

Dawn Office

Urban Plague

Dawn Office is a small but wealthy Grade 4 Office and an associate Office of the Liu Association. Run by Salvador, a veteran Fixer, the Office is located in V Corp's Nest.

Kurokumo Clan

Urban Plague

The Kurokumo Clan is a subsidiary Syndicate of The Thumb. Distinguishable by its achromatic colors, irezumi-style augmentation tattoos, and katanas, they seek to benefit from the collapse of Lobotomy Corporation by taking over the territory of the fallen Wing.

Shi Association

Urban Nightmare

The Shi Association is one of the Associations of the City, specializing in swift and covert assassinations. The Association strives to maintain a level of neutrality and fairness in its dealings, but in recent times it has grown corrupt and lost respect for the sanctity of death.

Smiling Faces

Urban Nightmare

The Smiling Faces are a Urban Nightmare-class Syndicate of cannibals with influence all across the City. Distinguishable by their grinning iron masks, rustic accents, and the smell of tobacco, these uncanny predators are dreaded by everyone who knows of their actions.

Liu Association

Star Of The City

The Liu Association is one of the Associations of the City, specializing in direct combat and warfare. Two Sections of the Liu Association's Southern Branch, Liu South Section 2 and Liu South Section 1, are fought in the Library as Guests.

Hana Association

Star Of The City

The Hana Association is one of the Associations of the City, acting as the head of all other associations and offices. The specific section fought in the Library is Hana South Section 3, lead by Director Mirinae.
